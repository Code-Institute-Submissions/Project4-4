For Food Vendors, how to get started?
Create an account.

Go to Vendor Access and add in your vendor profile.
Vendor profile will be pending verification after added.

Start adding foods to the vendor profile and customise your delivery reach based on postal codes or towns.

Once the vendor profile is verified by us, your customers can see and order your food in their home page.
For Food customers, how to get started?
Create an account.

Go to Home Page and create a profile.

Vendors and foods show according to you profile in home page.
